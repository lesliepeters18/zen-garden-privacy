# Zen Garden — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Zen Garden ("the app") is developed by Leslie Peters as a solo indie game for Android.

## Data we collect

- **Approximate location** (GPS, accuracy ~10m) — used only to place your plants on a map and find nearby gardeners. Stored on our server (Supabase, US-West region) tied to a random account ID, not your real identity.
- **Camera feed** — used in-session for AR plant placement. Camera frames are NOT recorded, transmitted, or stored.
- **Device identifier** — a random UUID generated on first launch, stored in Supabase to identify your garden across sessions.
- **Email (optional)** — only if you choose to link an email for account recovery. Used solely to send a 6-digit recovery code.

## Data we do NOT collect

- Real-time location (we never track you in the background)
- Photos or videos
- Contacts, calendar, messages, files
- Advertising identifiers or analytics IDs
- Any personal information beyond what's listed above

## Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent your data to any third party. Your data lives in Supabase (PostgreSQL, US-West region). No advertising networks, no analytics services.

## Data deletion

Open the app → Settings → Reset Progress → Clear Cloud Garden. This permanently deletes your row from our database. You can also email lesliepeters18@gmail.com to request deletion.

## Children

This app is rated for ages 7+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in-app and reflected here.
